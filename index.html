Cryptography

The QR code that got you here was an example of Stegnography.

This is a demonstration of how information can be hidden in plain sight. Cryptography isn't just about scrambling messages, it's about the control of information.

From the Enigma machine in WWII to the encryption that protects your messages today, the principle remains the same: ensuring that a message can only be read by those for whom it is intended.
